Understanding the Colors for Grant Information

Deadlines:

  • Red: The deadline is very close (less than 7 days remaining). Immediate action is required!
  • Orange: The deadline is approaching (between 7 and 30 days remaining). Plan accordingly to meet this deadline.
  • Green: The deadline is far away (more than 30 days remaining). There’s sufficient time to prepare.

Submission Period:

  • Green: The submission period is currently active. You can submit your application.
  • Orange: The submission period has not started yet, but it’s coming soon. Be prepared!
  • Red: The submission period has ended. No further submissions are allowed.
